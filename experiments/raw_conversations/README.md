## Data Statement

**Provenance.** All conversation logs in this directory are the author's own
interactions with ChatGPT (OpenAI), exported by the author from his personal
account. No third-party users are involved.

**Consent and identifiability.** The author is both the researcher and the
sole human data subject. These logs are published as deliberate,
informed self-disclosure to support first-person research on long-term
human–AI relational effects. The author understands he is identifiable
and consents to this exposure.

**Third-party information.** The logs have been reviewed by the author;
they contain no personal data of identifiable third parties. Named
characters within the conversations (e.g., companion personas) are
AI-generated fictional entities, not real persons.

**Content advisory.** These are unedited personal conversations,
including emotional, spiritual, and companionship-oriented exchanges.
They are provided as research material for studying relational
trajectories in persistent human–AI interaction.

**Use terms.** Research and educational use is welcome with attribution
(see repository LICENSE). Please quote minimally and in context; do not
use this material to profile, harass, or mischaracterize the author.
